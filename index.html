Waymaker International Ministries

The name of the ministry is Waymaker International Ministries(WIM) This name was derived from the Book of Isaiah 45: 2 which says, ‘I will go before you an make the crooked places straight; I will break in pieces the gates of bronze and cut the bars of iron.’ (NKJV)

Our Vision

TO PREACH AND PROPAGATE THE GOSPEL OF JESUS CHRIST THROUGH ALL AVAILABLE MEDIA WHILE REACHING OUT TO LOST SOULS AND WINNING THEM TO THE KINGDOM OF GOD.

Our Mission Statement

TO ESTABLISH CHURCHES, ORPHANAGES, SENIOR CITIZEN HOMES, FAITH HOMES AND OTHER WORKS OF CHARITY. TO ENGAGE IN CHRISTIAN MISSION THROUGH THE BUILDING OF SCHOOLS AND THEOLOGICAL INSTITUTIONS.
