The Ministry

Fashion is not something that exists in dresses only. Fashion is in the sky, in the street. Fashion has to do with ideas, the way we live, what is happening. Never, ever confuse what happens on a runway with fashion. A runway is spectacle. It's only fashion when a woman puts it on. well dressed hasn't much to do with having good clothes. It's a question of good balance and good common sense.
